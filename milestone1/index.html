Website Summary:

What content will my site contain?

This site will act as my digital portfolio, to track personal projects that I develop.

Where will I get all of the content for my site?

All text will be written by me. Images will come from projects or screenshots of said projects. Fonts/Icons and any external sources will be properly credited.

Why should users car about the focus of my site?

My portfolio will help employers, instructors and classmates to quickly assess what I can create. It also makes it easy to view all of my work in one place

Why should my site be built for this?

Building my site around a portfolio will allow me to create something that can serve me long after the completion of this introductory course. It will act as a professional way to show of my projects and skills, alongside giving contact information for future employers

What would I like users to do at my site?

I want users to browse my projects to view my previous projects and evaluate my skill level. With the option to contact me for collaboration or hiring opportunities.

What is the goal of my site?

To demonstrate my growth over time, showcase my previous projects and provide a simple way to learn about my skillset and reach out.
