Analysis of Portfolios

This section will provide an analysis of the competition in the field of web development and portfolio showcasing.

  • Example 1: Bruno Simons Portfolio

    Strengths: Highly creative and visually appealing, demonstrates advanced web development skills.
    Weaknesses: May be overwhelming for users looking for a more traditional portfolio, and may not be accessible to all users due to its heavy use of graphics and animations.

  • Example 2: Henri Heymans Portfolio

    Strengths: Very strong visual design and artistic approach. Incorporates interactive elements effectively.
    Weaknesses: Visuals maybe distracting or difficult to navigate for some users.

  • Example 3: Brittany Chiang Portfolio

    Strengths: Clean and modern design, easy to navigate, and effectively showcases a variety of projects.
    Weaknesses: May not stand out as much in a competitive field due to its more traditional design approach.
