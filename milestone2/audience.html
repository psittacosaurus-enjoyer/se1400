Who should use this site?

Geolocation

The primary audience for this site would be local customers in Southern Utah, especially people living in or near St. George who are looking for nearby automotive options.

Age

The main age range for this site would be adults from about 20 to 50 years old. This includes first-time car buyers, college students, working adults, and families looking for reliable transportation.

Gender

The site would be designed for all genders. However, the majority of users may be adults who are actively shopping for a vehicle for themselves or their family.

Users Who Want a Simple Shopping Experience

The audience for this site includes people who want a clean, friendly, and organized website that makes car shopping feel less overwhelming.
