Analysis of Competitors

  • Example 1: Autotrader.com

    Large national vehicle marketplace Strengths: Autotrader has a large selection of vehicles and strong tools for buying or selling cars.
    Weaknesses: Too many advertisments which can overwhelm site-goers. The site can feel broad and less personal because it is built for a national audience.

  • Example 2: Cars.com

    Vehicle marketplace and research site Strengths: Cars.com is useful for researching vehicles, comparing models, reading reviews, and estimating payments.
    Weaknesses: The amount of research tools and information may feel distracting, especially for new users who just want to browse cars quickly.

  • Example 3: .4seasonsauto.com

    Local Dealshimp Comptetitor Strengths: 4 Seasons Auto is a local dealership site focused on used vehicles, affordable payments, and financing help.
    Weaknesses: It has a smaller selection than national websites and is more limited to local dealership inventory. The scope doesn't make it a bad choice by any means, just means there is less variety available.
